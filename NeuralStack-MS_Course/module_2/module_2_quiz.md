### Module 2 Quiz

1. What does OWASP LLM01 refer to?
   a) Supply chain compromise
   b) Prompt injection
   c) Unbounded consumption
   d) Misinformation

2. Excessive Agency (LLM06) is broken into which three root causes?
   a) Confidentiality, integrity, availability
   b) Excessive functionality, excessive permissions, excessive autonomy
   c) Data, model, and infrastructure risk
   d) Reconnaissance, execution, and impact

3. Which category specifically concerns risks in retrieval-augmented (RAG) systems?
   a) LLM07 — System Prompt Leakage
   b) LLM08 — Vector and Embedding Weaknesses
   c) LLM05 — Improper Output Handling
   d) LLM02 — Sensitive Information Disclosure

4. What is the main purpose of MITRE ATLAS?
   a) To replace the OWASP Top 10 entirely
   b) To catalog adversary tactics and techniques specific to AI systems, modeled on ATT&CK
   c) To certify AI security engineers
   d) To define legal compliance requirements for AI

5. Unbounded Consumption (LLM10) is best described as:
   a) A model hallucinating facts
   b) An AI-era denial-of-service risk tied to inference cost and resource exploitation
   c) A type of supply chain attack
   d) A failure to validate model output before use

**Answer key:** 1-b, 2-b, 3-b, 4-b, 5-b
